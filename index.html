Site Name

This site is going to be called inlandvalley.com. Just as the name indicates, it would a website for the inland valley community and its businesses hence the name inlandvalley.com.

Domain name: inlandvalley.com

Site Purpose

The Inland valley chamber website is dedicated to creating a community of businesses in the inland valley community. Each business represents a member of the inland chamber of commerce. This organization is committed to promoting the business interests of the members of the organization and promoting the area, attracting additional businesses, tourism and people. Also, by clicking on the inland valley website, people can have an overview of the inland community, it's people, weather and businesses located in the inland valley community.
